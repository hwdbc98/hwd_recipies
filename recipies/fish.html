Fish

Description

There are plenty of reasons to get excited for warmer weather, but we’d say that grilling fish is pretty high up there. The best grilled fish recipes are deeply flavorful—plus delightfully quick to make and easy on the cleanup.

You probably already know that a touch of char can make the plainest salt-and-peppered fish fillet taste better. These recipes, though, deliver even more. You'll find the marinades and sauces that help salmon, halibut, and snapper shine, plus smart ways to tackle fish skewers, cedar plank grilling, and whole fish too.
